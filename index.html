The Revolutionary Idea

A government-run, open digital marketplace where ministries, states, districts, startups, NGOs, and citizen groups can buy and sell verified outcomes, not inputs.

üéØ The Problem We're Solving

Most government programs pay for activities ‚Äî trainings conducted, toilets built, devices distributed, saplings planted. But outcomes are what truly matter: jobs sustained, toilets used, learning improved, trees surviving, treatment completed.

NOM flips procurement from input-based to outcome-based at national scale.

How It Works

Four simple steps to transform governance

A

Government Posts Outcome Contracts

Ministries, states, or districts post contracts with clear targets:

  • Reduce dropout rate by X%
  • 85% TB treatment completion
  • Increase groundwater by Z cm
  • 70% waste segregation
  • Improve literacy by X points

Each includes: Target metric, time window, payment per result, standardized measurement method

B

Implementers Bid & Deliver

Startups, NGOs, SHGs, universities, or citizen consortia compete with:

  • Innovative approach
  • Cost per outcome unit
  • Proof of capacity

Best solution wins, not best connections.

C

Outcome Proof Rails Verify

Standardized verification using:

  • Existing government datasets
  • Third-party audits
  • Sample surveys
  • Geo-tagged proof
  • Tamper-resistant logs

Principle: Auditable and reproducible evidence

D

Payment Only for Outcomes

Results trigger payments automatically:

  • Reduces waste immediately
  • Rewards innovation
  • Exposes ineffective programs
  • Enables state-to-state comparison

No results = No payment. Period.
